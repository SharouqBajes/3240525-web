About the University of Jordan

The University of Jordan is the largest and oldest university in Jordan, founded in 1962. Located in Amman, it offers many programs in different fields.

Main Campuses:

  • Faculty of Engineering
  • Faculty of Science
  • University Library
  • Sports Complex
  • Student Center

Quick Facts:

Faculty of Engineering

The Faculty of Engineering has programs in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Computer Engineering. Labs and workshops are modern and well-equipped.

Departments:

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Computer Engineering
  • Architecture

Faculty of Science

Faculty of Science covers Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Geology with advanced research labs.

  2. Chemistry

University Library

The library is the main academic resource for students and faculty, offering books, journals, and digital resources.

  • Open daily 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Online databases and e-books available
  • Group study rooms
